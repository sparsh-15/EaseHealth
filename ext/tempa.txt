Test Data 1
Name: Paracetamol
Description: Used to relieve mild to moderate pain and reduce fever.
Ingredients: Paracetamol 500mg
Effective Body Part: Head, Body
Side Effects: Nausea, Liver damage (high dose)
Warnings: Do not exceed the recommended dose. Avoid alcohol.

Test Data 2
Name: Ibuprofen
Description: A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for pain relief and inflammation.
Ingredients: Ibuprofen 400mg
Effective Body Part: Joints, Muscles, Head
Side Effects: Stomach pain, Heartburn, Dizziness
Warnings: Avoid taking on an empty stomach. May cause kidney issues in high doses.

Test Data 3
Name: Amoxicillin
Description: An antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections.
Ingredients: Amoxicillin 250mg
Effective Body Part: Lungs, Throat, Urinary Tract
Side Effects: Diarrhea, Skin rash, Nausea
Warnings: Complete full course of medication. Avoid if allergic to penicillin.

Test Data 4
Name: Cetirizine
Description: An antihistamine used to relieve allergy symptoms.
Ingredients: Cetirizine 10mg
Effective Body Part: Nose, Eyes, Skin
Side Effects: Drowsiness, Dry mouth
Warnings: Avoid alcohol. Do not operate machinery if drowsy.

Test Data 5
Name: Metformin
Description: Used to control blood sugar levels in type 2 diabetes.
Ingredients: Metformin 500mg
Effective Body Part: Pancreas, Bloodstream
Side Effects: Stomach upset, Lactic acidosis (rare)
Warnings: Monitor kidney function. Take with food
